RAID, Volumes and Snapshots

RAID Types

  • RAID 0: striped, no redundancy, good performance
  • RAID 1: mirroed, redundancy
  • RAID 5: 3+ drives, good for reads, bad for writes. AWS strongly recommends not using RAID 5 on EBS.
  • RAID 10: Striped and mirrored, good redundancy, good performance.

If exam gives an option for using RAID 5 as an answer, it's NOT the answer. RAID 10 is great for IO.
